## Day 1 — Friday, 22 May: Arrival & St Helier deep-dive

**Theme: Settle in, oyster baseline, museum primer for Saturday's War Tunnels**

- **~09:40** Land Jersey (BA1346, scheduled 09:40 BST). Pick up hire car (~15 min to hotel)
- **~10:30** Drop bags at Grand Jersey concierge (room won't be ready until ~15:00). Sort parking, change shoes
- **~11:30** **Central Market + Beresford Street Fish Market** (10 min walk from hotel). Wander the Victorian iron-and-glass market, fountain, Annette's tea stall. Cross to the Fish Market opposite for a half-dozen Royal Bay oysters from a fishmonger — your oyster baseline before Saturday's Faulkner BBQ. *Ask if they'll shuck on the spot — not verified, may need to take away*
- **~13:00** **Lunch: The Yard** (friend-rec, all-day brunch, kitchen to 14:30). Walk over from the market
- **~14:30** **Royal Square + Liberation Square.** Royal Square is where Liberation was declared 9 May 1945. ~30-45 min potter
- **~15:30-17:00** **Jersey Museum + Maritime Museum** (waterfront, ~5 min walk from hotel). Sets up Saturday's War Tunnels with broader island/occupation context. Maritime Museum has the Occupation Tapestry — strong primer
- **~17:00** Hotel check-in proper, freshen up
- **~18:30** Aperitif (optional): **The Watchmaker** (43 Halkett Place, ~10 min walk) — sophisticated wine bar
- **~19:30 Dinner: Bohemia Restaurant** (Green Street, ~15 min walk). 1 Michelin star, Chef Callum Graham. ~£99 4-course; 7-course tasting available. Phone ahead re prawn allergy

> 🦐 **Allergy:** Phone Bohemia (01534 876500) ahead and confirm prawn cross-contamination protocol at booking and again on arrival.
>
> 🛏️ **Hotel alternative:** **Tassili at the Grand Jersey** (in-hotel, zero walk). Wed–Sat 18:30–21:00, tasting menu, no under-12s. Friend's strong rec — trade-off is Bohemia = bigger Michelin destination, Tassili = no taxi/walk after a long arrival day.

---

## Day 2 — Saturday, 23 May: West/North coast — war history, oyster lunch, cliffs, Corbière sunset

**Theme: The full-day driving day — no rush, big anchors**

- **10:00** Leave hotel. Drive ~15 min to **Jersey War Tunnels**, St Lawrence
- **10:30-13:00** **War Tunnels** — allow 2.5-3 hrs (tunnels + exhibitions + Liberation café + war-walk garden). Adult £21 / Senior £20 (2026)
- **~13:30** Drive ~20 min to **Faulkner Fisheries BBQ** at L'Étacq for Jersey oysters (£2 each), scallops, lobster off the BBQ. Tue–Sat 12:00–15:00, no bookings
- **~15:00** Drive ~15 min to **Plémont** car park. Walk the **Plémont → Grève de Lecq cliff stretch out-and-back** — most dramatic short slice of north-coast path. ~1 hr out, ~1 hr back. Wide sea views, headlands, wildflowers
- **~17:15-18:30** **Plémont Bay caves.** Steep ~200 steps to the beach. Sat low water 19:08 — caves, waterfalls, rock pools open. Steep steps back up
- **~18:30** Drive 15-20 min to **Corbière Lighthouse**
- **~18:50-19:45** **Corbière causeway walk** (low water 19:08, sunset 20:55 — walking out in good light, no sunset wait). **Re-confirm Ports of Jersey causeway calculator the morning of.** Bring a layer — breezy headland
- **~20:15** Back to hotel (~25 min)
- **~20:45 Dinner: Banjo** (8 Beresford Street, ~10 min walk, kitchen to midnight). European brasserie in restored Victorian gentlemen's club. Market menu £24.95/£29.95. Civilised hour, short walk

> 🦐 **Allergy:** Banjo's menu has a Goan prawn curry — phone (01534 850890) ahead and confirm prep separation. Also call **Faulkner Fisheries (01534 483500)** the day before re shared-BBQ prawn cross-contamination.
>
> 🌧️ **Weather fallback for Faulkner:** Le Moulin de Lecq pub at Grève de Lecq, or The Priory Inn at Devil's Hole.

---

## Day 3 — Sunday, 24 May: East coast — Mont Orgueil + light walk + spa

**Theme: One major anchor, one easy stroll, then unwind**

- **10:00** Leave hotel. Drive to **Mont Orgueil**, Gorey (~25 min). Park at Gorey Pier (free Sun)
- **10:30-13:30** **Mont Orgueil full experience** — fortress, dungeons, top-of-keep views to French coast. ~200 steps; wear good shoes. Adult £19.50 / Senior £18.50 (2026). *Live during our trip: Summer Night Sessions started Fri 22 May*
- **13:30-15:00** **Lunch in Gorey.** Pick: **Castle Green Gastropub** (closest, harbour view), **Bass & Lobster** (up the hill, Royal Bay seafood — book ahead), or **The Crab Shack** (casual harbour terrace)
- **15:00-16:30** **Gorey → Anne Port out-and-back cliff stroll** (~1.5 km each way, easy-moderate, sea views). Finishes at your car. No taxi, no shuffle
- **~17:00** Drive back to hotel (~25 min)
- **17:00-19:15** **Spa + downtime** at the Grand Jersey. Real 2+ hr session
- **19:30 Dinner: The Grill at The Royal Yacht** (Weighbridge, ~3 min walk from Grand Jersey). Steak/seafood institution dating to 1807 — Donald Russell dry-aged steaks, Jersey lobster, sea bass, scallops. Walk-ins to The Grill itself; bookings via the Grill Lounge

> 🦐 **Allergy:** Phone The Royal Yacht ahead — confirm prawn cross-contamination protocol. Lamb on the mixed grill (avoidable).
>
> 🛏️ **Sunday dinner constraint:** Tassili at Grand Jersey closed Sun; Sirocco no Sun dinner; Samphire closed Sun — all ruled out for tonight.
