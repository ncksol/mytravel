# Restaurant Recommendations

The trip has three signature dinners (Fri/Sat/Sun) and an oyster-focused lunch on Saturday at Faulkner Fisheries. Friend-recommended alternatives are listed for swap flexibility — Sunday is the constrained night because Tassili, Samphire and Sirocco are all closed.

> 🦐 **Allergy thread (partner):** Confirm prawn cross-contamination protocol at every venue, both at booking and on arrival. Avoid mutton-forward menus (Nick).

---

## 🍷 Dinners

### Bohemia Restaurant — Friday 22 May

- **Where:** Green Street, ~15 min walk from Grand Jersey
- **Style:** 1 Michelin star, Chef Callum Graham. ~£99 4-course; 7-course tasting available
- **Hours:** Friday dinner 18:30–21:15
- **Reservation:** 01534 876500
- **Allergen note:** High-end kitchen, strong allergen handling — confirm prawn at booking
- **Hotel alternative:** **Tassili at the Grand Jersey** (in-hotel, zero walk). Wed–Sat 18:30–21:00, tasting menu, no under-12s. Friend's strong rec. Trade-off: Bohemia = bigger Michelin destination; Tassili = no taxi/walk after a long arrival day

### Banjo — Saturday 23 May

- **Where:** 8 Beresford Street, next to Central Market, ~10 min walk
- **Style:** European brasserie in restored Victorian gentlemen's club. Sister to Oyster Box
- **Hours:** Mon–Sat 11:00–24:00
- **Menu:** Market menu £24.95 / £29.95. Oysters, sea bass, scallops, steak
- **Reservation:** 01534 850890
- **Allergen note:** Reviewers note attentive allergen handling. Menu has Goan prawn curry → confirm prep separation

### The Grill at The Royal Yacht — Sunday 24 May

- **Where:** Weighbridge, ~3 min walk from Grand Jersey
- **Style:** Steak/seafood institution dating to 1807. Donald Russell dry-aged steaks, Jersey lobster, sea bass, scallops
- **Hours:** Open Sundays for lunch & dinner
- **Reservation:** Walk-ins to The Grill itself; bookings via the Grill Lounge
- **Allergen note:** Phone-confirm prawn cross-contamination at booking. Lamb on the mixed grill (avoidable)
- **Why Sunday:** Tassili at Grand Jersey closed Sun, Sirocco no Sun dinner, Samphire closed Sun — The Grill is one of the few strong Sunday options walkable from the hotel

## 🦪 Lunches

### Faulkner Fisheries BBQ — Saturday 23 May

- **Where:** The Vivier, L'Étacq, St Ouen
- **Style:** Open-air seafood BBQ. Jersey oysters (£2 each), scallops, lobster
- **Hours:** Tue–Sat 12:00–15:00, weather-dependent. No bookings
- **Phone:** +44 1534 483500
- **Allergen caveat:** Shared BBQ → **call ahead the day before** re prawn cross-contamination
- **Weather fallback:** Le Moulin de Lecq pub at Grève de Lecq, or The Priory Inn at Devil's Hole

### The Yard — Friday 22 May

- **Style:** All-day brunch (friend-rec)
- **Hours:** Kitchen to 14:30
- **Why:** Walking distance from Central Market — sets up the Friday afternoon waterfront-museum block

### Gorey lunch options — Sunday 24 May

Pick one after Mont Orgueil:

- **Castle Green Gastropub** — closest, harbour view
- **Bass & Lobster** — up the hill, Royal Bay seafood — book ahead
- **The Crab Shack** — casual harbour terrace

## 🔁 Friend-recommended swap options (Fri/Sat only — both closed Sun)

### Samphire

- **Where:** 7–11 Don Street, ~10 min walk
- **Hours:** Tue–Fri 11:00–late, Sat from 09:30–late, **closed Sun & Mon**
- **Style:** Fancy contemporary British/European, rooftop terrace, ~£26–40 mains
- **Friend's pick:** "fancy" tier — could swap Bohemia (Fri) or Banjo (Sat)

### Awabi

- **Where:** 63 Halkett Place, ~10 min walk
- **Hours:** Tue–Fri 12:00–14:00 + 17:30–22:00; Sat 12:00–22:00; **closed Sun & Mon**
- **Style:** Pan-Asian wine bar / late-night-drinking-den vibe. MICHELIN Guide listed
- **Friend's pick:** "best deep-fried sea bass" — could swap Banjo (Sat) for a less-formal middle night

### Roseville Bistro (Maybe)

- **Where:** Havre des Pas, ~20 min walk / 5 min drive
- **Style:** High-rated seafood
- **Hold as:** Saturday alternative to Banjo

- **Where:** 43 Halkett Place, ~10–12 min walk from Grand Jersey
- **Style:** Sophisticated, intimate. Strong reputation
- **Hours:** Mon–Wed 12–22, Thu–Sat 12–23, **closed Sunday**
- **Use as:** Friday or Saturday aperitif
