# Menton Trip Itinerary — 27 June – 5 July 2026

## At a Glance

| Dates | Base | Nights | Shape |
|-------|------|--------|-------|
| Sat 27 Jun – Sun 5 Jul | 28 Avenue de la Riviera, Menton (central seafront) | 8 | Work week + 2 free days + Nice arrival |
| **Total** | | **8 nights · 9 days** | Nick + girlfriend |

---

> 💼 **Working week:** both work the daytimes Mon 29 Jun – Fri 3 Jul. Evenings only, low energy. The **two free days (Sun 28 Jun + Sat 4 Jul)** carry the trip.
>
> 🏊 **Daily ritual:** swim every day, morning before work and evening after. Closest beaches (Borrigo/Casino/Marché, ~1.3 km) are rocky; **Fossan (1.7 km) or Sablettes (2.1 km, sandy)** are the swim spots. Lifeguards only July–August — early-week morning swims are unsupervised.
>
> 💷 **FX:** £1 ≈ €1.16 (ECB, 26 Jun 2026). Prices shown £ first, € in brackets.

## Day 1 — Saturday, 27 June: Arrival + 6 hours in Nice

**Theme: Land, store the bags, do Vieux Nice slowly, train on to Menton**

- **09:45** Land Nice (BA8475 from London **City**, not Heathrow — Terminal 1)
- **~10:15** **Tram T2** airport → Nice centre. AERO ticket £8.60 (€10) return pp — also covers your Sun 5 Jul return airport leg
- **~10:45** Drop bags at **Bounce – Nice Station (Retail)**, 3 min from Gare Nice-Ville. £8 (€9) for both bags. **Pre-book online**
- **11:00** **Cours Saleya market** — Saturday = flower + produce + fish market (not the Monday antiques day). Socca / pissaladière from the **Chez Thérésa** stall (~£3, market mornings to ~13:00)
- **11:25** Gentle Vieux Nice loop: Cours Saleya → Place Rossetti → Palais Lascaris exterior (15 rue Droite)
- **12:30 Lunch: Le Bistrot d'Antoine** (27 rue de la Préfecture). Traditional Niçoise, mains £16–25, 3-course ~£31–40pp. **Reserve: +33 4 93 85 29 57** — 26 covers, fills fast
- **14:00** Quai des États-Unis → **Colline du Château** via the **free lift at 1 Rue des Ponchettes** (park free, 08:30–20:00). 360° bay views, easy in the heat
- **~15:30** Collect bags → **TER Nice-Ville → Menton** (~35–40 min, £7/€8 pp)
- **~16:30–17:00** Arrive Gare de Menton → ~5–10 min walk to the flat. Evening swim + easy local dinner

> ⚠️ **Flight is London City (LCY), not Heathrow** — check your UK-airport logistics.
>
> 🥘 **No-reservation lunch fallback:** Chez Pipo (13 rue Bavastro, Port area, Sat 11:30–14:30; socca £3–7). *Avoid Oliviera — permanently closed.*

## Day 2 — Sunday, 28 June: FRENCH DAY — Monaco + Èze 🇲🇨

**Theme: Glamour and a medieval eagle's-nest village — no car, all rail/bus**

- **~11:00** TER **Menton → Monaco-Monte-Carlo** (~12 min, ~£3.40/€4)
- **~11:30** Up to **Monaco-Ville / Le Rocher**. **Changing of the Guard — 11:55 daily**, Place du Palais
- **~12:00** **Prince's Palace** (£11.20/€13; 10:00–18:00, last entry 17:15 — Italian-Renaissance frescoes + Grands Appartements) + Saint-Nicholas Cathedral (free) + the old-town lanes
- **~13:15 Lunch: Castelroc** (1 Place du Palais) — terrace facing the Palace, traditional Monégasque. ~£52–65pp (€60–75) with a glass of wine — well under your £100 cap. **Reserve: reservation@castelroc.mc**
- **~14:30** TER to **Èze-sur-Mer** + **Bus 83** up the hill (or Bus 602 from Monaco)
- **~15:00–16:45 Èze Village**: medieval lanes, Chapelle Sainte-Croix, Notre-Dame church → **Jardin Exotique d'Èze** (£8.60/€10; daily 09:00–18:30; cactus garden + 429 m viewpoint)
- **~17:00** Back to Menton via Monaco. Evening swim + light dinner

> 🍝 **Choose Palace over Oceanographic Museum** (£19/€22.50): the Palace's architecture/frescoes fit your taste better and keeps the day light. Don't try to do both.
>
> 🚫 The old "Marché de la Condamine food hall" lunch idea is **out — the hall is shut for renovation all 2026.** Castelroc replaces it.

## Days 3–7 — Monday 29 June – Friday 3 July: Working week

**Theme: Swim → work → swim → easy local dinner. Low effort.**

A verified 5-night dinner rotation (closing days handled, each Google-Maps status-checked):

- **Le Stanley** — Place du Cap. Provençal/seafood, **open 7/7** (good for Monday when others shut). ~£34pp (€39)
- **La Trattoria** — 15 Av de Verdun (closest to base). Sicilian-Italian, ~£22pp (€25). Closed Mon
- **Bar Brasserie Rino** — 29 Rue Saint-Michel, old town. French/Italian value ~£9–17pp (€10–20). Closed Fri — the easy/light night
- **Carnival** — 29 Quai de Monleon, Vieux Port. French-Italian bistrot, ~£24pp (€28)
- **Pecora Negra** — Quai Gordon Bennett, at Sablettes beach. Colagreco's Neapolitan pizza, ~£22pp (€25). Closed Tue — combine with a sunset swim

Evening / free-morning culture (see **Sights**): Cocteau Bastion, Serre de la Madone, Basilique Saint-Michel, Jardin Biovès.

> 🍽️ Backup dinner: **ABAM** (5 Rue du Vieux Collège) — superb food but closed Sun+Mon and mixed service reports; book ahead.

## Day 8 — Saturday, 4 July: ITALIAN DAY — Ventimiglia + Dolceacqua 🇮🇹

**Theme: Cross the border for Ligurian texture + Monet's bridge — then home for the festival**

- **~09:45** TER **Menton → Ventimiglia** (~16 min, ~£2.60/€3 — carry passport, occasional border checks)
- **~10:30** **Riviera Trasporti Bus 7** Ventimiglia → Dolceacqua (~13 min, €2–3; catch on Via Cavour, ~7 min from station)
- **~11:00 Dolceacqua**: cross the **Monet-painted Ponte Vecchio** (free) → carrugi up to the **Castello dei Doria** (£5.20/€6; summer Sat 10:00–13:30; Monet film + panoramic walkway)
- **~12:45 Lunch: Casa e Bottega** (Piazza Garibaldi 2) — **Michelin Bib Gourmand**, 4.5★ (1,268 reviews). Order the **coniglio al Rossese** (rabbit in the local red — Michelin signature) + a glass of **Rossese di Dolceacqua DOC**. ~£52–65pp. **Book: +39 0184 205038**
- **~14:30** Dolceacqua old town wander → Bus 7 back → TER to Menton
- **Evening — Fête de la Saint-Pierre** (Menton, free): the seafront is programmed. **20:30–23:00 electro night on Quai Eugénie**, walkable from base — roll into it after the train

> 🎉 **Lucky timing:** Menton's Fête de la Saint-Pierre lands on 4–5 July, right on your dates. Sat night electro on the seafront; Sun morning Mass + procession + boat blessing.
>
> 🚫 **Skip Hanbury Gardens** — lovely but too much for a slow day already doing Dolceacqua. *Ventimiglia's big market is Friday only — Saturday has none; Ventimiglia Alta old town is the substitute.*

## Day 9 — Sunday, 5 July: Departure (festival morning)

**BA8468 departs Nice 17:00.** A relaxed morning is possible.

- **Morning** Festival continues: **10:00 Solemn Mass at the Basilica → procession → 12:00 blessing of boats → 12:30 free apéritif** at the capitainerie. One last morning swim before you pack
- **~13:30** Leave Menton by TER → Nice → **Tram T2** to the airport (check-in closes 16:00)
- **17:00** Depart BA8468 to London City (arrives ~18:00)

> ⚠️ **BA8468 has a poor punctuality record** (avg ~51-min delay). Keep the return buffer comfortable but don't panic if it slips.

## The one splurge: L'Orangerie

A weeknight (Tue–Fri), once you've got the energy.

- **L'Orangerie** (3 Rue de la Marne, central Menton). **Michelin Bib Gourmand** — Mediterranean-Japanese "semi-gastronomique". **£34 (€39) 3-course** or **£65 (€75) chef's tasting**. Closed Sun+Mon; Tue–Sat 12:00–13:30 & 19:00–21:00. **Book online (Zenchef)**
