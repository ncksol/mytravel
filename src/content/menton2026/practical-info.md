# Practical Info

No hire car — everything runs on the coastal train, a couple of buses, and walking. Menton is the still point; Monaco, Èze, Ventimiglia and Dolceacqua are all short hops on the line.

> 💷 **FX:** £1 ≈ €1.16 (ECB, 26 Jun 2026). All prices £ first, € in brackets.

---

## ✈️ Getting in and out

- **In:** BA8475 from London **City** (LCY — not Heathrow), lands Nice (NCE) Terminal 1 **09:45** Sat 27 Jun
- **Out:** BA8468 NCE → LCY, departs **17:00** Sun 5 Jul (T1, check-in closes 16:00)
- ⚠️ **BA8468 punctuality is poor** (avg ~51-min delay on recent rotations) — keep the return buffer comfortable

## 🚆 Airport ↔ Nice ↔ Menton

- **Tram T2** NCE → Nice centre (~30 min). **AERO ticket £8.60 (€10) return pp** — also covers the Sun 5 Jul return leg
- **TER coastal train** Nice-Ville → Menton: ~35–40 min, **£7 (€8) pp**, ~every 20–30 min
- Alight at **Gare de Menton (main)** — NOT Menton-Garavan. ~5–10 min walk to 28 Ave de la Riviera through Jardin Biovès
- Tickets at station machines (card/coin) or the SNCF Connect app

## 🚌 Excursion transport

- **Menton ↔ Monaco-Monte-Carlo:** TER ~12 min, ~£3.40 (€4)
- **Èze Village:** TER to Èze-sur-Mer + Lignes d'Azur **Bus 83** up the hill; or **Bus 602** (runs Sundays — 5 trips), €2.20
- **Menton ↔ Ventimiglia:** TER ~16 min, ~£2.60 (€3) — **carry passport** (occasional border ID checks)
- **Ventimiglia ↔ Dolceacqua:** Riviera Trasporti **Bus 7** ~13 min, €2–3; catch on Via Cavour ~7 min from Ventimiglia station. Saturday = weekday ("feriale") service, so it runs

---

## 🏖️ Beaches & swimming

- Closest beaches (Borrigo/Casino/Marché, ~1.3 km) are **rocky** — bring water shoes
- **Fossan (1.7 km)** or **Sablettes (2.1 km, sandy)** are the better swim spots
- **Lifeguards July–August only** — late-June early/late swims are unsupervised; check the day's flags when surveillance starts (~1 July)

---

## 💶 Money & costs

- **Monaco** is a tourist-price trap — the £100pp lunch cap is deliberate; Castelroc lands ~£55pp, U Cavagnetu ~£35pp food-led
- **Italy** is good value — the Ligurian lunch at Casa e Bottega is quality without a blowout
- Everyday Menton dinners run £9–34pp depending on the spot
- Carry some cash for market socca stalls and bus tickets

---

## 📌 Day-of checklist

- Re-pull TER + bus times the morning of each excursion (SNCF Connect; Lignes d'Azur / Riviera Trasporti sites)
- Re-confirm BA8468 24h before
- Check the Fête de la Saint-Pierre programme week-of (4–5 July; schedule can shift slightly)
- Pre-book the Bounce luggage drop online before arrival day

---

## ☀️ Season notes

- Late June / early July: warm, long evenings, sea swimmable. Pack for heat + sun
- The two free days carry the trip — keep them for the excursions; weekday evenings stay near base
- One splurge dinner (L'Orangerie) is sanctioned; everything else value-led
