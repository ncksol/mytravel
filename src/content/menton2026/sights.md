# Sights & Things to Do

Menton is the base; the two free days reach into Nice + Monaco (France, combined Sunday) and Ventimiglia/Dolceacqua (Italy). Weekday culture is gentle and close to home — gardens, the Cocteau Bastion, the old town. Everything below is hours/price-verified (27 Jun 2026) with closing days flagged.

> 🏛️ **Pace rule:** cap each anchor at ~2 hours. Few excellent things over a checklist.

---

## 🏖️ Daily swims (the trip's backbone)

Both swim morning and evening, every day.

- **Closest beaches** to 28 Ave de la Riviera (~1.3 km / 15-min walk west): Borrigo / Casino / Marché — but **narrow and rocky (galets)**
- **Best for a daily swim:** **Plage de Fossan** (1.7 km / ~18 min) — wide, sheltered, sand-pebble mix, calm shallow water; or **Plage des Sablettes** (2.1 km / ~25 min, past the old town) — the **only sandy-ish beach**, shallow, family-friendly
- The Promenade walk to them is itself a Riviera pleasure

> ⚠️ **Lifeguards only July–August.** Your Sat 27 Jun–~30 Jun early-morning and late swims are unsupervised — fine for confident swimmers, just know the flag system isn't running yet.

## 🌿 Menton gardens & culture (weekday evenings / free-day mornings)

### Musée Jean Cocteau — Le Bastion
- Quai Napoléon III, on the seafront. Daily 10:00–12:30 & 14:00–18:00, **closed Tuesday.** £4.30 (€5)
- 🎁 New exhibition *"Le Château de la Bête"* opens **4 July** — runs your whole trip
- *(The Wunderman Cocteau museum stays storm-closed since 2018 — the Bastion is the one to see.)*

### Serre de la Madone
- 74 Rte du Val de Gorbio (bus 7 from the gare). **Tue–Sun 09:00–18:00, closed Monday.** £8.60 (€10)
- Lawrence Johnston's "Jardin Remarquable" — a lovely free-day morning

### Val Rahmeh — botanical garden
- Garavan side, walkable from Garavan station. Closed Tuesday, 10:00–18:00, ~£6–8 (€7–9)

### Jardin Maria Serena
- 21 Promenade Reine Astrid. **Guided tour only**, Tue/Thu/Sat 10:00–11:30, £8.60 (€10). **Pre-book +33 4 93 28 35 94**

### Basilique Saint-Michel-Archange & Jardin Biovès
- **Basilique** — the baroque centrepiece of the old town. Free
- **Jardin Biovès** — central public garden, free, ~1 km from base (the Fête du Citron site in winter)

> 🕕 Most gardens close ~18:00, so they fit free-day mornings better than after-work.

## 🏎️ Sunday combined day — Nice morning + Monaco F1 (Sun 28 Jun)

### Nice (morning)
- **Cours Saleya market** — Sun flower + produce, 06:30–13:30 (socca at Chez Thérésa)
- **Free SIM borne** — Fnac / Maison de la Presse on av Jean Médecin (UK card OK); CAP3000 fallback (open Sun 11:00–19:00)
- **Vieux Nice** — Place Rossetti, Cathédrale Sainte-Réparate, Palais Lascaris exterior
- **Colline du Château** — free lift at 1 Rue des Ponchettes (08:30–20:00), 360° bay views

### Monaco F1 (afternoon) 🏎️
- **Prince's Car Collection** — 54 route de la Piscine, Port Hercule. ~100 cars incl. **F1 cars from the Monaco GP + Monte Carlo Rally cars** (newest a 2013 Lotus F1), Ferraris, Bugattis. £13 (€15) adult; open daily incl. Sun, 10:00–18:00
- **Walk the GP circuit** (free, self-guided, ~4.4 km, easy) — Sainte-Dévote → Casino Square → Mirabeau → **Fairmont (Station) Hairpin** → Portier → **the Tunnel** → Nouvelle Chicane → Tabac → Swimming Pool → Rascasse. (GP was 4–7 Jun — grandstands long gone, streets clear)
- *Optional if you'd rather: Prince's Palace State Apartments (£11.20/€13) or Oceanographic Museum (£19/€22.50) — but the F1 pair is the point of the day*

> 🚫 **Èze dropped** from this trip — redundant against Dolceacqua + Ventimiglia Alta below, and mobbed by 10:30.

## 🇮🇹 Italian day — Ventimiglia + Dolceacqua (Sat 4 Jul)

### Dolceacqua
- **Ponte Vecchio** — the 15th-c bridge Monet painted (1884). Free, always accessible
- **Castello dei Doria** — £5.20 (€6); summer Sat 10:00–13:30 (Jul–Aug 10:00–19:00). Monet film + Doria rooms + panoramic walkway
- The medieval Terra quarter carrugi + the Rossese vineyards on the slopes

### Ventimiglia
- **Ventimiglia Alta** — the medieval upper town: narrow carrugi, Romanesque cathedral, panoramic terrace
- *The famous seafront market is **Friday only** — Saturday has none. Ventimiglia Alta is the Saturday substitute*
- **Skip Hanbury Gardens** (La Mortola) this trip — beautiful but too much for a slow day with Dolceacqua

## 🎉 Fête de la Saint-Pierre (4–5 July — on your dates, free)

Menton's summer-opening sea festival, right across your Italian-day evening + departure morning.

- **Sat 4 Jul:** 14:00–18:00 Splash Party at Sablettes · **20:30–23:00 electro night, Quai Eugénie** (walkable from base)
- **Sun 5 Jul:** 10:00 Solemn Mass at the Basilica · 11:30 procession · 12:00 blessing of boats · 12:30 free apéritif at the capitainerie · 23:00 boat-burning (subject to authorisation)
- Throughout: free kayak, paddle, diving, sailing, e-foil
